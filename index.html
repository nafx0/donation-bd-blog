Common FAQs

FAQ 1: How can I make a donation?

Answer: You can make a donation by clicking the "Donate" button on our homepage. Choose the cause you wish to support, enter your donation amount, and follow the instructions to complete your transaction securely. We accept various payment methods for your convenience.

FAQ 2: Where does my donation go?

Answer: Your donations go directly to supporting various charitable initiatives in Bangladesh, including disaster relief, educational programs, and health services. We ensure that a significant portion of your contribution reaches those in need. For more details on our projects, please visit our "Projects" page.

FAQ 3: Can I track my donation?

Answer: Yes! After making a donation, you will receive a confirmation email with details about your contribution. You can also log in to your account on our website to view your donation history and the impact of your contributions.
